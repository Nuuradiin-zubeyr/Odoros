Emergency Water Trucking Coordination

39 Days
10 Hours
46 Minutes
50 Seconds

This strategic session focuses on mobilizing water tankers to the most drought-affected rural areas. We will discuss logistics, funding, and real-time data monitoring to ensure that emergency water reaches families in need as quickly as possible. Your participation is vital for a coordinated national response

Our goal is to optimize the distribution network by connecting private water suppliers with the most vulnerable communities identified by the Odoros platform. By attending this session, you will gain access to the latest drought vulnerability maps and help us prioritize life-saving deliveries for the upcoming weeks

Why join this event !

our presence matters. By joining this coordination event, you become part of a structured response that ensures emergency water reaches the most vulnerable households in Somalia quickly and transparently.

Direct Relief

Ensure that every liter of water donated reaches the specific village and family it was intended for through our tracking system.

Collective Power

Work alongside local authorities, NGOs, and community leaders to avoid duplication and maximize our limited resources.

Save Lives

Your contributions go directly towards fuel for water tankers and the maintenance of strategic community boreholes.

Community Resilience

Help us build long-term solutions so that these communities can better withstand future droughts without external aid.
